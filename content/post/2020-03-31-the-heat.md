# The Heat

```
The heat of the sun beckons me.
My summers share the fate of fire;
Burn. Fry. Die.
I embrace it. Rehabilitation through
debilitation.
I let the thick, hot air
w r a p
     i t s e l f
          a r o u n d
       m y
b o d y
and cleanse my heart of
stale moistures.
```
